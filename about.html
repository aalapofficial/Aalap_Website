ABOUT US

We Together Make "Aalap "!

#EXPLORE Yourself


The first club formed in KMIT, AALAP – The Music Club of KMIT visions in exploring the various new aspects of music, as the tagline says #EXPLORE YOURSELF. AALAP provides the right platform to all music aspirants and enthusiasts in not only showcasing their talent, but to also explore, innovate and implement such ideas. From live performances to interactive jams to online collaborations, AALAP loves to take challenges and expand it’s wings, reach out to more and more people and keep entertaining them in the World of Music.

#AUDITIONS


Every year auditions are conducted which is open to all the students of the college and will be judged by a panel of senior proficient artists. The members of the club would be selected based upon their interest, eagerness to be a part, talent, rhythmic sense and the ability to catch up a scale and perform. Those who are decent enough, have the minimum knowledge required in music and skill in their genre are selected as interns. These interns will be trained for a certain period of time and based in their performance, they will be converted into club members.

#FOUNDERS


Aalap was found on 20-08-2018.
