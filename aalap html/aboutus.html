About Aalap

Aalap is all about exploring yourself through music.


Aalap was found on 20-08-2018.
Aalap is KMIT's first ever music club which aims to elevate the music scenario in the college by encouraging music enthusiasts , performers and professional musicians by getting them all under one roof. Aalap was founded by Ritvik Oruganti , Chintan and Nikhilesh
